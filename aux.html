Aux Send Shitty Limits' story. Limits story. Auxbout. There is not much to explain. The name is hewn roughly from these referents: a public-television Saturday hour devoted Central Texas song and story; a box to modulate the dynamic range of a line-level signal fed into it and out again; everything that is no good to anyone. The name names an episodic practice whose elements are chance, inattention, and a faint but perennial hope that from chance and inattention and no directed effort some interesting interval will be born.

But what in practice does that mean. Almost nothing. A transducer can go one way--sound to electricity--or the reverse. Start with the one. We do. Pick a reliable, costly one. Pick an unreliable, low-cost one. Form a tandem. Listen to what happens. Maybe nothing. Probably nothing. Try something different. Remember that there is no sound source. That explains non-results.
